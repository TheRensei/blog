## Overview:
Investigators get invited to a private auction. After a brief mingling with the guests, the action starts and to their surprise it turned out to be a trap! Stuck in a ritual where the host is trying to feed the unseen power, will they find a way to stop the ritual and escape the loop?

## Backstory:
Gordon wants power and starts researching occult. Finds some fucked up thing with snakes and decides to perform the ritual. The ritual succeeds but not in the way he expected. He gained power, but lost his humanity. The snake lives in his cane and is controlling him like a puppet. The mansion is created with the snakes power which is running out as it needs constant sustenance. 

The snake requires human sacrifices but only of murderers. The way to achieve it is to make new murderers! The two cursed items are used to cause one person to stab another one. When that happens the snake just eats the murderer.

---

## Twist:
As with anything occult - there is a surprise - the items used in the ritual have unintended consequences. Every time a person kills/dies, the time loops to the beginning of current event. Gordon is aware of the time loop, and the butlers know something is up. None of the guests have any idea about the loop unless they are stabbed wearing the necklace. 

## The start:
GM's job is to ensure the necklace is bought by one of the PCs. And that the dagger is bought by any NPC.

## Timeline

#### Before the auction
Before the auction starts - players need to get to the mansion. Driving on their own, hiring a taxi or even walk, establish that beforehand as it'll define their possible way out!
Whatever mode of transportation they choose - they arrive before 6pm. All other guests are already present - outside of the mansion there is a couple cars visible. Once PCs arrive they are greeted by the butlers and invited inside. The party is started - they are welcome to drinks and food on the table in one of the front rooms. It's a great opportunity to mingle with some of the guests.

Another room with access is a 'show room' where the owner of the mansion is showing off their collection. Various artefacts, ancient texts, tablets, tools etc, some even of occult nature. All protected by glass cases preventing PCs from investigating closer. Another good opportunity to introduce a couple of NPCs and attempt to make conversation.

No one was told about the specific items that would be auctioned today, or who the owner is exactly - nothing besides rumours.

After about 30 minutes the guests are greeted by the host - Gordon - who after a short introduction invites everyone into a separate room where the action will be performed.

There is a long dim corridor leading to the room. One of the butlers leads the way in as all of the guests follow.

The room is covered with red velvety material covering all walls and reaching down to the floor. In the middle of the room there are 5 rows of chairs for the guests. At the back of the room is a stage on which the action items will be presented. There are two long curtains on the sides obscuring doors on the left and right side of the stage. They are not visible from the start but getting close to the stage reveals them.

Ask PCs to roll an extreme POW roll when they get inside the room. Nothing happens when they fail, but if they succeed they have sudden bad feeling. The goal of this is just to build a little bit of tension.

Each seat has a name on it and PC names are in the 4th row from the stage. 

Ezra almost faints and slumps on the floor when crossing the doorway. Lee tries to help her immediately and asks one of the butlers for water. He assures people who are worried (especially PCs) that Ezra is fine and he'll help her sit down. After a minute the butler brings her a glass of water.

#### The auction

There is one butler standing in the entrance door. Gordon comes up on the stage and gives a short talk about the party, himself and todays auction. He mentions the shared interest in the artefacts of a specific nature between all guests.

The auction starts with an old manuscript about farming. It's a mundane item that's just there as a buffer item. It can be used to introduce some more NPCs present in the room as they try to win the item, either an NPC or a player wins the item when it reaches the price of $200.

The second item is the dagger. It should start high around $100. Make sure to note it's usage in rituals to pique PCs interest, but NPCs will fight to win this item no matter what. It shouldn't land in player's hand, not this time. The NPC that's got the item is then asked to inspect and appreciate the item up close on the stage. They are enamoured by it, they cannot take their eyes off it. 

Gordon moves on, NPC with the dagger still on the stage is standing to one side. Another item is a large golden necklace of two intertwining snakes. The necklace is big enough that when worn there is an empty space in the middle of the wearer's chest.  Gordon also highlights the fact of the occult nature of the item. The item's price is surprisingly low. Ask players to roll normal Occult, Antics or some related skill. They can't tell much about the item itself, but that low of a price for an item of this kind is unheard of, unrealistic, but the deal is too good to miss. If PCs try to get the item - other NPCs don't try to fight them too much, or more friendly NPCs even whisper towards the PCs that they'd be willing to give up on this item if PCs are willing to consider skipping the next one. 

Make sure one of the PCs wins the item. They are then asked by Gordon to go get on the stage and inspect their item. As they are stood next to the previous person holding the dagger - Gordon comes in the middle of the stage and says "ONE" in a deep, dark voice - towards the crowd. He then says a short chant (mentioning the 7 victims), an eldrich command. When that happens the person holding the sword stabs the PC in the heart. Roll some dice - ask the player for their HP. With a face full of terror let them know that the damage was equal to their HP. Describe the feeling of the dagger piercing their heart and their body slump on the floor.

*the loop starts*

At that time Ezra almost faints and slumps on the floor. Lee gets close to her and lets everyone know that she's fine, asks one butler for some water. 

Everything proceeds the same way with ONE change - the NPC who won the dagger is gone. If PCs ask their acquaintances (if any are present) they have no memory of them and are unwilling to talk more about this - they try to avoid the topic. 

The auction proceeds as before

### Items:
- invitation letter with a wax seal. The seal has an outdated and unused logo of the local printing company. The letter invites PCs to a private action. It states the time for 6pm on Saturday, the location is the mansion. There is also a map on how to get there from the main road. PCs were invited because of their known interest in the antics and stuff. The letter is written in beautiful cursive with gold framing and very good quality paper.
